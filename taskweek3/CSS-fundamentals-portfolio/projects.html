Xtracto

Xtracto: Xtracto is the safe, secure, fast and reliable software to easily upload image, PDF, CSV, and JSON files, mp3, mp4 etc of any size and extract their metadata. It displays extracted metadata and allows users to save, download, and share files and metadata. This wonderful work was achieved by I and my team of great minds from I4G & ZURI scholarship.

Library

Django REST framework (DRF) is a powerful and flexible toolkit for building web APIs. In this project, a library application with Django CRUD operations was developed where you can keep track of all your books by uploading new books, viewing, updating, reading and deleting them.

Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game with many names and variations. It is commonly used as a way of coming to decisions. The rules require that competing players use one hand to form one of three shapes at an agreed-upon time. The person that plays the strongest “object” is the winner of the game.
